About Us

Welcome to our state-of-the-art Surveillance System,an advanced solution designed for optimal security and monitoring.Developed as a final year project under the guidance of Prof. J.A. Opadiji,this system integrates cutting-edge technology to offer remote control and monitoring through a seamless web application. Built with Django, HTML,tailwind CSS, and JavaScript, it allows you to manage your surveillance setup from anywhere.Featuring real-time video livestreams, intuitive button controls for adjusting camera angles and recordings,comprehensive data logs, and precise timestamps, our system ensures you are always aware and in control of your premises.Experience reliable and efficient security with our innovative Surveillance System.
